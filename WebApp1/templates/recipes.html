*How to make brownies:

  1. Have Ruth make them; she does it the best. If she can't see the next step.
  2. Purchase two boxes of Betty Crocker Fudge Brownies.
  3. Yes, seriously Betty Crocker has it perfect. Trust me, from scratch isn't worth it.
  4. You'll also need eggs and vegetable oil and whatever it says on the box. I can't remember anymore.
  5. Yes, get the super "unhealthy" oil. If someone tries to give you "healthy" brownies, RUN AWAY!
  6. Follow the instructions on the box! This is baking not cooking.
  7. Yes, butter the pan.
  8. Stir the ingredients as little as possible to mix everything together. Do not whip it; you will make cake.
  9. The bake timing is always wrong, so start low and you can always keep in the oven a LITTLE longer.
  10. When the brownies are done in the oven, they should look well cooked on the edges, not burnt, and use a toothpick to check the middle. It's alright if there is a little brownie on the toothpick. You're not making cake, but lets face it you probably made cake.
  11. Let the brownies cool for at least 20 minutes. I know they smell amazing, but you will burn your tongue and make a huge sticky mess if you do not wait.
  12. Ok, now you can eat them, but it's best served with Turkey Hill Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream.
  13. No, the whole pan is not for you. Sharing with family and friends is required.
